Trip 1: Rapids Adventure

Join us for an exciting adventure through the roaring rapids of the White River. This trip is perfect for thrill-seekers looking for an adrenaline-packed experience. Navigate through challenging rapids and enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding wilderness.

Trip 2: Family Fun Day

Bring your family along for a fun-filled day on the river. Our family-friendly trip is suitable for all ages and skill levels. Enjoy a leisurely float down the calm stretches of the river, with opportunities for swimming and picnicking along the way.

Trip 3: Sunset Expedition

Experience the magic of the river at sunset on our evening expedition. This trip offers a unique opportunity to witness the beauty of nature as the sun sets over the water. Paddle through calm waters and enjoy the tranquility of the evening.
