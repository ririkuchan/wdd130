Rafting Site Plan

Ririku Shirakawa

Overview

Purpose

To design a detailed layout of the rafting site, including key features such as river access points, camping areas, safety measures, and recreational facilities. This plan serves as a blueprint for organizing and managing rafting activities, ensuring the safety and enjoyment of participants while preserving the natural environment.

Audience

The primary audience for the Rafting Site Plan includes rafting guides, outdoor recreation enthusiasts, safety officials, and environmental conservation organizations. Additionally, stakeholders such as local government agencies, landowners, and tourism boards may also review the plan to ensure compliance with regulations, promote sustainable tourism, and maximize economic benefits for the community.

Style Guide

Color Palette

When selecting colors for the Rafting Site Plan, it's essential to consider clarity, readability, and visual appeal. A harmonious color palette can enhance understanding and communication of the plan's elements. For example, natural tones like greens and blues may represent vegetation and water bodies, while contrasting colors such as reds or yellows could highlight important safety features or landmarks. Additionally, using muted or earthy colors can evoke a sense of the outdoor environment and blend well with the surrounding landscape. Overall, the chosen color palette should facilitate easy interpretation of the plan while reflecting the adventurous and natural aspects of rafting activities.
